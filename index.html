About

Holy Cow Studio is located in the scenic landscapes of Kamloops, British Columbia, Ran by Emilio Pagnotta a multifaceted creative force, seamlessly blending the roles of recording engineer, producer, and musician. With an innate passion for sound and an unwavering dedication to music, Emilio has carved out a unique niche in the Interior BC music scene
